Blueberry Cheesecake

Ingredients

    For crust
  • 2 1/3 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unstalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sugar
    • For filling
  • 4 8-ounce packages cream cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 16-ounce container sour cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
    • For topping
  • 2 6-ounce baskets fresh blueberries
  • 1/3 cup all-fruit blueberrt spread

Preparation

    Make crust
  1. Position rack in center of oven and preheat to 375 F
  2. Wrap outside of 10-inch-diameter springform pan with heavy-duty foil.
  3. Combine all ingredients in processor and blend using on/off turns until crumbs are moist
  4. Press crumb mixture firmly onto bottom and 2 inches up sides of prepared pan.
  5. Bake until crust begins to brown, about 8 minutes, and transfer crust to rack and cool.
    Make filling
  1. Using electric mixer, beat cream cheese and sugar in large bowl until well blended.
  2. Wrap outside of 10-inch-diameter springform pan with heavy-duty foil.
  3. Beat in sour cream, milk and vanilla.
  4. Pour filling into crust.
  5. Place springform pan in large roasting pan.
  6. Pour enough hot water into roasting pan to come 1 inch up sides of pan.
  7. Bake cheesecake until just set in center and top is slighlty puffes and golden brown.
  8. Turn off oven; keep door closed; let cheesecake stand in oven for 1 hour.
  9. Remove cheesecake from roasting pan and refigerate until cold.
  10. Cover; refrigerate overnight.
    Make topping
  1. Stir clueberry spread in large skillet over low heat until melted.
  2. Remove from heat and add blueberries.
  3. Spoon blueberry mixture over top of cake, spreading evenly.
  4. Chill until cold and transfer cake to platter.
