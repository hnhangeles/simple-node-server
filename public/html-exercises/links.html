Top Picks: A Blog of My Bests and Greatests


My Top 3 Movie Picks

The third spot goes to My Blue Valentine . This movie is one for the books because it is interestingly styled and shot. It features outstanding performances from Ryan Gosling and Michelle Williams and an inventive screenplay and structure.

The second spot goes to Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind. This film is absolutely stunning. Works marvel after marvel in expressing the bewildering beauty and existential horror of being trapped inside one's own addled mind, and in allegorising the self-preserving amnesia of a broken but hopeful heart. At its core, Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind could have been just another love story. Refracted through Kaufman's wonderfully weird prism, it's something truly memorable

The top spot goes to Celeste and Jesse Forever. This movie has quite a differemt tune to it; thus, my great admiration to it. With its refusal to follow the usual romantic-comedy formula, this snappy and observant movie is a nice surprise. There is quiet, seditious verve to the way expectations are derailed and then re-railed, just as in those areas of real life that still exist even outside the expanding Sundance empire of middlebrow observationalism.


The Top 3 Songs of My Playlist


The third spot goes to If I Die Young. This song never fails to give me goosebumps. It reminds me of how our lives could go by in just an instant. Thus, to live a life of great adventure is what the song tries to convey. Indeed, beautiful.

The second spot goes to See You Again/Love Me Like You Do/Sugar. This is actually an acoustic mash-up of three songs. I love YouTube covers and this is just absolutely stunning. I love how the YouTube artists were able to blend the three songs in such a way that not one song overpowers the other. It takes great talent to do that; thus, kudos to this awesome cover!

The top spot goes to Blank Space/Jealous. Another mash-up takes the top spot - granted that I love two of these songs seperatley, it just gave me utmost happiness that these songs were able to come together beautifully. Also, plus points because I absolutely adore both Taylor Swift and Nick Jonas!
